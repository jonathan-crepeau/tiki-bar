HAPPY HOUR

TIKI BAR is a Tiki bar located in the Financial District of San Francisco housed in a historical space that was once home to the infamous Tiki Bob's Mainland Rendezvous. As you enter TIKI BAR you find yourself in the belly of a wooden ship that will transport you off the streets of San Francisco and deliver you to an exotic escape.
